# B1: Coding Without a Map

Before specs, teams often code based on assumptions.

<div class="bad-practice">
<strong>Bad Practice</strong><br>
Prompt: "Build a shopping cart module."<br>
Copilot starts coding, but key requirements are unclear: guest checkout, discount rules, tax logic, inventory, currency.
</div>

| Metric | Vibing | Spec-Driven |
|---|---|---|
| Rework | High | Lower |
| Team alignment | Low | High |
| Review clarity | Low | High |

<div class="exercise">
<strong>Try it now</strong>
<ol>
<li>Ask Copilot to build a shopping cart module.</li>
<li>List at least 5 assumptions made by the generated solution.</li>
</ol>
</div>
